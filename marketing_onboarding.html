Marketer Onboarding

Your First 30-Day Plan

Your 30-Day Mission

Your primary focus is to build foundational momentum for **F-Bridge Africa**. Your measurable goal for the next 30 days is to generate **15 qualified leads** for our high-value services (Tech, Media, or Operations) and secure our **first 2 new clients**.

Performance Expectation:

Please note, achieving the goal of generating 15 qualified leads and securing 2 new clients within your first 30 days is a key performance indicator for this role. Failure to meet this foundational target may result in the cancellation of your contract.

Your Role & Weekly Objectives

Your Role: Digital Marketer

Your role is to execute our growth strategy by taking our brand message to our target audience. You will focus on high-impact activities that generate leads and build our community. Here is the plan for your first four weeks:

Week 1: Immersion & Content Distribution

  • Get familiar with our new brand voice, our **4-Pillar service model (Tech, Media, Ops, Capital)**, and our target customer.
  • Take approved content (posts, flyers, articles) and begin sharing it across LinkedIn and relevant Facebook Groups.

Week 2: Community Engagement

  • Actively participate in 3-5 online communities (Facebook/WhatsApp groups, Nairaland) by providing helpful, expert answers to represent our brand.
  • Engage with comments and replies on our own social media posts.

Week 3: Direct Outreach

  • Begin executing personalized outreach campaigns on LinkedIn to connect with 10-15 potential clients daily.
  • Focus on starting valuable conversations, not just selling.

Week 4: Reporting & Feedback

  • Create a simple report on which content, channels, and messages are getting the most engagement.
  • Provide feedback and insights to help us refine our strategy for the next month.
